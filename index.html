About Us

We are THE
BOSS FOUNDATION.

Founded in 2024 in Kodambakkam, Chennai — a community-first NGO and education platform dedicated to transparent giving and career-launching internships for the next generation.

Our Mission

Serving people.
Building futures.

We believe charity must be transparent and education must be accessible. THE BOSS FOUNDATION operates at the intersection of social service and skill development — running ground-level NGO operations while mentoring students through real-world internships.

Every action is documented, photographed, and shared — because trust is everything.
